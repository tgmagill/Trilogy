At Trilogy Residential Management, the health and safety of our residents, team members and communities always comes first. We have added many safeguards to our operational processes which are continuously evolving to ensure the safety and wellbeing of our residents and team members, while still delivering many of the services that you expect as a Trilogy resident. We have put together the following answers to many questions that we have received regarding COVID-19

Resident FAQ

 Is the leasing and management office still open? 

Our teams are still on-site, but we continue to ask that you communicate via phone, email or through our resident app.
We are currently offering virtual tours and self-guided tours at and are happy to provide you with more details on how you can live at our wonderful community. Please schedule a tour on our main website and our teams will reach out to you with more details on our current tour process.   

 Can I put in a work order to repair something in my home? 

We are still performing essential maintenance work orders and will continue sharing DIY videos to help with some of the more simple issues that you may be experiencing. We are asking all residents to still self-quarantine in another room while our maintenance team performs the necessary work and to wear a face mask while our team is in your home.

 Are the common areas, like mailboxes, package rooms and elevators being cleaned more frequently? 

We have added more extensive cleaning and sanitizing throughout the common areas of the community. 

 How do I get my packages? 

We are no longer accepting packages in the office. All packages will be directly delivered to your doorstep by the courier or by our team if the courier is unable to deliver directly. The only exception to this is if your community has a separate package room or package locker area. We ask that you do not visit the office to retrieve packages.

 Are there any programs to help with my rent payment? 

Trilogy is paying for all credit card fees for the month of May should you choose to use your credit card to pay your rent. Should you need additional assistance, please contact the office.

 I am unsure if I want to renew my lease, but I also don’t want to move out right now, what are my options? 

We are currently offering residents flexible lease options and renewal incentives. Please contact the office for more details.

 Are the amenities open? 

While we have closed our amenities and suspended in-person resident events, we are offering alternative virtual events to keep our residents involved and create a sense of community on our social channels. These events include fitness classes, maintenance tips, cooking classes and community activities while practicing social distancing.
